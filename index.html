What you receive

The complete
racing service.

Most tipster services send you a selection. Race of the Day sends you a selection, the reasoning behind it, the results from yesterday, one-click links to place your bet, and a weekly summary of how the year is going. Everything in your inbox, every morning, before the first race.

📧

The daily email

Every morning, before racing begins, your email arrives with that day's selection - or a note explaining why no selection has been made, which is equally useful information. The email includes the race, the horse, the advised price and the reasoning behind the pick.

Alongside the day's selection, yesterday's results appear automatically. You open one email and you know exactly where you stand.

🔗

Embedded bet links

Every selection comes with one-click bet links to Betfair, Sky Bet, Paddy Power and other major bookmakers. You do not copy odds into a search bar. You do not hunt for the race on your bookmaker's site. One click and you are at the bet slip, at the right price, before the market moves.

This is the feature that separates Race of the Day from every platform-based service in this space. No other independent tipster service at this price point does it.

📱

WhatsApp delivery

The day's selection also arrives via WhatsApp, simultaneously with the email. If you are not near a desktop when racing begins, the selection is already in your pocket. No app to download. No login to remember. Just a message, with the pick, before the off.

If a selection is amended during the day - late market moves can occasionally change the picture - the WhatsApp message is updated accordingly.

📊

Weekly P&L summary

Every week, a summary arrives: your running points total for the year, the current win and place rates, and a note on the races worth watching in the week ahead. Race of the Day is not a daily tipping sheet that happens to arrive every morning. It is a complete racing intelligence subscription.

The record is cumulative, transparent, and always available to read - before you subscribe, after you subscribe, and every week in between.

The methodology

The intelligence
behind the selection.

The selection process starts by eliminating 90% of the day's races before a single horse is looked at. Form, ground conditions, draw bias, trainer patterns, market confidence - all feed into a filter that most races fail to pass. What is left is a card of races where the edge is real enough to act on.

The intelligence behind the selections comes from 60 years of racing experience - as a mathematician who understands probability, as an owner who has stood in the parade ring and watched how horses behave before they run, and as a commentator who has called thousands of races and learned to read a field in real time.

There is no algorithm generating these selections. There is no model being trained on historical data. There is a person who has spent six decades learning to find races where the price in the market does not reflect what the evidence says should happen.
